Providing Physical Therapy in Buffalo, New York Welcome to Family Care Physical Therapy

Family Care PT is a family-oriented Physical Therapy clinic consisting of a highly-educated, professionally trained staff who utilize expert knowledge and technique to create an ideal healing environment. We believe a genuine environment reflecting care, understanding, and response to the needs of each patient will lead to the return of optimal health, and we strive to create this type of environment each and every day. Family Care Physical therapy has locations in Buffalo and Williamsville.

Our Promise To You

Family Care Physical Therapy operates on one simple principle: to provide personalized, one-on-one care to each and every one of our patients. We believe a genuine environment reflecting care, understanding, and response to the needs of each patient will lead to the return of optimal health, and we strive to create this type of environment every day.

Our goal is to help our patients improve their health and regain their highest level of function in work, sport, and daily life through accurate diagnoses, therapeutic exercises, physical therapy, management of the dysfunction, and prevention education.

-FCPT Staff
